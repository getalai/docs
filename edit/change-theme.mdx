---
title: Change Theme
description: 'Choose a different theme for your presentation'
---

Alai provides you with a range of themes to choose from. Each theme comes with predefined colors and fonts.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open 'Edit with Alai' Panel">
    To start changing the theme, open the 'Edit with Alai' panel and select the 'Change Theme' option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a New Theme">
    Browse through the available themes and select one that best fits the style and tone of your presentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply to All Slides">
    Click on 'Apply to all slides' to update the entire presentation with the new theme you have selected.
  </Step>
</Steps>
