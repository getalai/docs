---
title: Insert Slide
description: 'Insert a slide anywhere in your presentation'
---

![insert-slide.gif](../images/insert-slide.gif)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open 'Edit with Alai' Panel">
    Access the 'Edit with Alai' panel and choose the 'Insert Slide' option to begin adding a new slide to your presentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Slide Details">
    Provide any optional instructions or context that will help in creating the content for your new slide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Insert the New Slide">
    Click on 'Insert Slide' to add the new slide after the slide that is currently selected in your presentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Slide Layout">
    Utilize the 'Change Layout' option to modify the layout of your newly inserted slide as desired.
  </Step>
</Steps>
