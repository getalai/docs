---
title: Rewrite Section
description: 'Learn how you can rewrite a specific section without affecting the rest of the slide'
---

Rewrite a specific part of your slide without modifying any other text.

![rewrite-slide-section.gif](../images/rewrite-slide-section.gif)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open 'Edit with Alai' Panel">
    Begin by opening the 'Edit with Alai' panel and select 'Rewrite Section' to focus on a specific part of your slide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Highlight Text Box">
    Click on the text box you wish to edit to highlight it, then select 'Get Text' in the Rewrite section panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Instructions">
    Enter your instructions into the panel, such as "Make the explanation simpler," to guide the rewriting process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Rewrite'">
    Press the 'Rewrite' button to initiate the update and modify the selected slide section accordingly.
  </Step>
</Steps>
