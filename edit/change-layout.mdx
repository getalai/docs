---
title: Change Layout
description: 'Choose from a wide range of layouts for your slide'
---

Change layout will transform the content of the current slide to fit the layout you want, rewriting as needed.

You can add additional instructions to make the transformation specific to your needs.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open 'Edit with Alai' Panel">
    Start by opening the 'Edit with Alai' panel and select the 'Change Layout' option to begin the layout transformation process.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Layout">
    Select from the list of available layouts that best suits the content and style of your current slide.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Optional Instructions">
    Type in any optional instructions to tailor the layout change to your specific needs, such as "Add another point about X."
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply New Layout">
    Click 'Change Layout' at the bottom of the panel to update your slide with the new layout.
  </Step>
</Steps>
