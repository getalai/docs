---
title: Change Theme
description: 'Choose a different theme for your presentation'
---

Alai provides you with a range of themes to choose from. Each theme comes with predefined colors and fonts.

1. Open the 'Edit with Alai' panel, select 'Change Theme'
2. Select the theme you like from the dropdown
4. Click 'Apply to all slides' to change the theme of the entire presentation.
