---
title: Rewrite Section
description: 'Learn how you can rewrite a specific section without affecting the rest of the slide'
---

Rewrite a specific part of your slide without modifying any other text.

1. Open the 'Edit with Alai' panel, select 'Rewrite Section'
2. Click on the text box you wish to edit to highlight it, then select 'Get Text' in the Rewrite section panel.
3. Type in your instruction, like "Make the explanation simpler."
4. Click 'Rewrite' to update the slide.
