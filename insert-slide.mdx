---
title: Insert Slide
description: 'Add a new slide to your presentation'
---

Insert a slide anywhere in your presentation.

1. Open the 'Edit with Alai' panel, select 'Insert Slide'
2. Add optional instructions and context for your new slide.
3. Click 'Insert Slide' to add the new slide after your currently selected slide.

You can change the layout of your newly inserted slide using the 'Change Layout' option
