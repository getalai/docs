title: Billing
description: 'Mange your payment settings'
---

You can manage your account settings in the 'Create new presentation' modal.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Account Settings">
    Access 'Account Settings' from the 'Create new presentation' modal to manage your billing options.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Manage Your Subscription">
    Choose to either 'Upgrade' your account for more features or 'Manage Account' to update your subscription details or cancel your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>
